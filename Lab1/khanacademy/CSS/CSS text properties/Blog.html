Visit Tashkent!

Posted on the 1st of June

On May 31, I flew to Tashkent, and on the night of June 1, I was already at my parents' house. We have been in Tashkent for two weeks. During this time, I met with my friends from Tashkent, walked with my younger sisters, visited restaurants, cafes and parks. I also visited the mountains of Tashkent with my aunt, her friend, and my brother and cousin.

Visit Shymkent!

Posted on the 16th of June

After that, we came to Shymkent to visit our grandparents. My cousins are there. We often walked with them in parks and shopping malls. We also went to the water park twice. We spent the whole day at the water park, swimming, eating, going down the slides and sunbathing. It was a lot of fun.
