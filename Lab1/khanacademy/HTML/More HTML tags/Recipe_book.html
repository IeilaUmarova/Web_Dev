Recipe #1

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: PIZZA
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat flour 500 g
Dry yeast 10 g
Salt 1 teaspoon
Sugar 1 tablespoon
Water 300 ml
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Tomato sauce by choice
Sausage 300 g
Cheese 300 g
Tomatoes 1 piece

Steps:

  1. Sift the wheat flour
  2. Add dry yeast. Mix it up
  3. Add salt and sugar to the flour
  4. Make a well in the flour, pour in the olive oil and gradually add water
  5. Knead the dough, cover with plastic wrap and put in a warm place for 1 hour
  6. Cut the sausage into small pieces
  7. Grate the cheese on a coarse grater
  8. Cut the tomato into thin slices
  9. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and use a silicone brush to brush a little sunflower oil
  10. Roll out the dough into a baking sheet, form small sides and grease with tomato sauce
  11. Lay out the sausage and tomato slices in layers
  12. Sprinkle liberally with cheese. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees and bake for 25 minutes until cooked
  13. Divide the finished pizza into portions. Enjoy your meal!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: AIRY THIN PANCAKES WITH MILK
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2.6% 500 ml
Eggs 2 pieces
Wheat flour 1½ cups
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
Sugar 3 tablespoons
Salt pinch
Baking powder on the tip of the knife

Steps:

  1. Pour the milk into a suitable container at room temperature, beat in the eggs, add salt and sugar
  2. Gradually add the flour, while stirring to avoid lumps. They will still turn out, so you need to stir them well. Bring to the consistency of low-fat sour cream. Add baking powder
  3. Stir everything, leave for 15-20 minutes and then add the vegetable oil. By the way, you can leave the pancake batter in the refrigerator and cook the pancakes later. Nothing will happen to him
  4. Pour a little oil into a very hot frying pan and fry the pancakes

Recipe #3

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: MILLET PORRIDGE
Ingredients Quantity
Millet 200 g
Water 500 ml
Milk 300 ml
Butter 50 g
Sugar by choice
Salt ¼ teaspoon

Steps:

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Rinse the millet with water to get rid of excess gluten. Pour into boiling water
  4. Add salt and sugar
  5. Cover and cook over low heat for about 20 minutes.
  6. When the cereal has absorbed all the water, pour in the milk.
  7. Cook the porridge, stirring, for another 5-7 minutes
  8. Remove from heat and add pieces of butter to the porridge
  9. Cover with a lid and let stand for 20 minutes.
  10. Serve the millet porridge, adding another piece of butter to the plate if desired.
